Slid Not Stirred
The fast-paced sidescroller where you platform as a glass sliding along the bar. The winning game of IGDA WPI's Cutthroat Game Jam, which had the theme Don't Stop! (~30 participants) The main thing I learned from this game was how to be flexible and to adapt within tight deadlines. We were given some fun restrictions like all sound effects originating from our voices, a prominent MS Paint animation, and more! - Level Design - Player damage handling, game systems programming - QA - Audio remastering Jacob Antepli (jaantepli@wpi.edu): Lead programmer and designer Sarah Bodkin (sbodkin@wpi.edu): Art
Zach Adams (zadams@wpi.edu): Art

Created in Unity
